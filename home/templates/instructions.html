📘 How to Use the TrackIt

Welcome! Here's a quick guide to help you get started:

  • 🆕 Create a habit with the button below the calendar. Your habit will appear above the calendar in a container.
  • 📅 Click on your habit's container to activate the calendar. Click a day on the calendar to create a log (Note: future days cannot be logged).
  • 🛠️ Use the edit and delete options on created habits to update or remove habit info.
  • 🏆 Each habit logs XP for its chosen category. Stack habits in the same category to earn more XP and level up faster!
  • 💰 Leveling up earns you coins, visible on the navigation bar. Use coins in the shop to buy items with special in-game benefits.
  • 🎒 Purchased items will appear on your profile. Be strategic with when you choose to activate your special items!
  • 🌐 Visit the community page to follow other accounts and view their favourite habits. Friend requests will appear in your inbox.
  • 🤖 Check the recommendations page for personalised habit suggestions!
  • 🔍 These instructions can always be found again in the nav bar if you get stuck.
